Who are We

Welcome to the official website of the STEM TEAL class of Dee Hwa Liong Academy. This website was created to showcase the students, teachers, class officers, and academic achievements of our class.

Our goal is to present our class in a modern and organized way while highlighting the hard work, cooperation, and dedication of every member of STEM TEAL.

Our Mission

To promote collaboration, innovation, and academic excellence among students while strengthening our skills in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Our Vision

To become a group of future innovators, scientists, and professionals who contribute positively to society through knowledge and teamwork.
